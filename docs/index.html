Role-aware accountability built in.

AI should support decisions, not make them automatically. BedFlow AI is designed with strict human-in-the-loop workflows.

  • ✓ Immutable task events and audit logs
  • ✓ Signed local identities and role permissions
  • ✓ Mandatory rationales for clinical overrides
  • ✓ Explainable ML feature importance snapshots
🛡️

Safety Boundary & Data Provenance

BedFlow AI is an educational decision-support demonstration. It does not diagnose, authorize discharge, replace clinical judgment, or connect to a live EHR. No Protected Health Information (PHI) is utilized. Models are trained on a hybrid of synthetic operational scenarios and de-identified public diabetes readmission datasets.
